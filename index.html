Kings World Incorporated

A helping Hand to the World through Education. An NGO with the focus of making quality and sound education accessible to individuals from diverse backgrounds.

About US

Kings World Incorporated (KWI) is a non-profit organization with a focus on making quality and sound education accessible to individuals from diverse backgrounds. It aims at making the world a better place through education. As Nelson Mandela said “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world”. The power of education extends beyond the development of skills, we need for economic success but also contribute to nation-building and reconciliation.

Founder and President

Harold Tobias Adu-Twum is the founder and President of Kings World Incorporated. He is an education enthusiast and a data professional with nearly four years of experience in the education, data, and technology sectors. Harold has made significant contributions to the education field through teaching, research, and mentorship, with a notable impact on how technology shapes the educational sector. With in-depth knowledge in data science, artificial intelligence, and higher education, Harold has mentored numerous students and has made significant strides in demonstrating how educational technology can improve student outcomes and make education accessible and equitable for students from diverse backgrounds.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To ensure that every student has equal access to quality education, empowering them to achieve academic excellence and uphold moral integrity.

Vision: Creating a world where economic background and inequality does not determine a student's success by: Providing essential resources for quality education to both teachers and students. Offering mentoring and guidance to support student development. Promoting the value of education to foster lifelong learning and personal growth. Inspiring hope and a brighter future for all students.
